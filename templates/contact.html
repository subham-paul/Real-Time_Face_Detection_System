Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the face detection?

Our technology achieves 99.2% accuracy in optimal conditions, with robust performance across various lighting and angle scenarios.

Can I use this for commercial projects?

Yes, our technology is available for commercial use with appropriate licensing. Contact our sales team for pricing details.

What browsers are supported?

We support all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge with WebRTC capabilities.

Is my data secure?

All processing happens locally in your browser. No images or personal data are stored on our servers.

Can I detect multiple faces simultaneously?

Yes, our system can detect and count multiple faces in real-time with no practical limit on the number of faces.

Do you offer custom solutions?

Absolutely! We provide custom implementations and enterprise solutions. Contact our partnerships team.
