How Our Technology Works

01

Video Capture

Utilizes WebRTC to access your device's camera and capture real-time video streams with optimal resolution and frame rate.

02

Frame Processing

Converts video frames to grayscale and applies preprocessing techniques to enhance detection accuracy and performance.

03

Face Detection

Employs Haar Cascade classifiers with optimized parameters to identify facial features and patterns in each frame.

04

Analysis & Counting

Processes detection results, eliminates false positives, and provides accurate face counting with bounding box visualization.

05

Real-time Display

Renders processed frames with visual feedback and updates face count statistics in real-time for immediate user feedback.
